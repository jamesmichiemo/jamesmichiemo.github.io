StartupBus 2013

This is gonna be so awesome! Yesterday, I got the news that I’ve been accepted to join the StartupBus crew for 2013. Once again, I’ll be making my pilgrimage over to Austin for SxSW. This’ll be my 4th trip to the conference, but this time I’ll be joining a team to create a startup on our way there.

The StartupBus is basically an annual event where teams of strangers on a bus travel together to conceive, build and launch startups. Each team only has 72 hours to create a startup that’s ready to present to potential investors at the end of the trip.

Right now I’m really psyched to be a part of this and I’m very thankful for this opportunity to collaborate with others to do great things.
